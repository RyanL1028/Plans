Shibuya Shinjuku

Shibuya

Lunch/Brunch: Flippers Shibuya (Pancakes)

Opening Hours: 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Address: 〒150-0041 東京都渋谷区神南１丁目１５−5, Jinnan Plaza Bldg., 1F

Website: Flipper's Website

How to Get There:

  • Walk 8 minutes to Tokyo Station Yaesu Exit.
  • Take JR Yamanote Line to Shibuya Station (~20 minutes).
  • Exit via Hachiko Gate, then walk 5 minutes to Flippers.
  • Note: You must arrive earlier than 11:30 otherwise you won't have enough time for other events today!

Early Noon: パンパンチュチュ代官山

Opening Hours: 11:30 AM - 5:00 PM

Address: 15-2 Daikanyamacho, Shibuya, Tokyo 150-0034, Japan

Website: パンパンチュチュ's Website

How to Get There:

  • Return to Shibuya Station and enter through Hachiko Gate.
  • Take Tokyu Toyoko Line to Daikan-Yama Station (1 stop, ~3 minutes).
  • The image below is a reference of the station map,
  • Take the exit on the right of the "Triangle"
  • Walk 5 minutes to パンパンチュチュ.

Shinjuku

Afternoon: Disney Flagship (Shopping)

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Address: Japan, 〒160-0022 Tokyo, Shinjuku City, Shinjuku, 3 Chome−17−5, T & T III, 地下１階～２階

Website: Disney's Website

How to Get There:

  • Take Tokyu Toyoko Line back to Shibuya.
  • If the train terminates and Shibuya, change to Platform 5 or 6 towards Wakoshi.
  • If the next, next-next or next-next-next train is a S-Train,
  • Take the S-Train, it skips most of the stations!
  • Take the train to F13 or Shinjuku-Sanchome
  • Exit via Isetan District Gate, Exit B6 to directly arrive at Disney Flagship Store.
  • If google says B5, it is also OK but you won't directly arrive at the shop.

Late noon: Takashimaya (高島屋) Times Square (Shopping)

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Address: 〒151-0051 東京都渋谷区千駄ケ谷５丁目２４−2

Website: 高島屋's Website

How to Get There:

  • Walk for 5 minutes and exit at E8

MIGON Shinjuku Ace Shop (Mini-Croissant)

Opening Hours: 11:00 AM - 9:30 PM

Address: 小田急エース, 南館, 新宿区西新宿１丁目 西口地下街 1号, Tokyo 160-0022, Japan
